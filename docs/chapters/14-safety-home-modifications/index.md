# Safety and Home Modifications

## Summary

This chapter addresses critical safety concerns and practical home modifications to create a safer environment for someone with dementia. You will learn comprehensive fall prevention strategies and how to conduct a home safety assessment. The chapter covers specific modifications including installing grab bars, using non-slip flooring, ensuring adequate lighting throughout the home, securing door locks to prevent wandering, and implementing stove safety measures. You will also learn about medication management to prevent errors, emergency preparedness planning, and the use of medical alert systems. These safety measures help reduce the risk of accidents and injuries while supporting independence and peace of mind for both the person with dementia and their caregivers.
