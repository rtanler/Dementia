# Therapeutic Interventions

## Summary

This chapter explores the wide range of non-pharmacological therapeutic interventions that can improve quality of life for people with dementia. You will learn about cognitive stimulation therapy, occupational therapy, physical therapy, and speech therapy as professionally delivered interventions. The chapter also covers complementary approaches including music therapy, art therapy, reminiscence therapy, validation therapy, reality orientation, sensory stimulation, pet therapy, aromatherapy, and massage therapy. These therapies address emotional well-being, maintain functional abilities, reduce behavioral symptoms, and provide meaningful engagement. Understanding these options helps families create comprehensive care plans that go beyond medication to support the whole person.
