# Daily Living and Caregiving Skills

## Summary

This comprehensive chapter provides practical guidance for supporting a person with dementia through activities of daily living (ADLs). You will learn specific techniques for assisting with personal hygiene, bathing, dressing, feeding, toileting, and mobility, including safe transfer techniques. The chapter covers establishing daily routines, managing sleep hygiene, meal planning, nutrition and hydration management, dental care, and grooming. You will also learn about incontinence management, skin care, proper positioning to prevent pressure sores, and comfort measures. These practical skills form the foundation of quality dementia care at home.
